\begin{align*} \dee{W}&=\left(1000\pi(1-y^2)\,\dee{y} \text{kg}\right)\times\left(3-y \text{m}\right)\times\left(9.8 \text{m}/\text{sec}^2\right)\\ &=9800\pi(y^3-3y^2-y+3)\,\dee{y}\text{ J}\\ \end{align*}

Then the work involved pumping out the entire tank to a height of 3 metres is:

\begin{align*} W&=\int_0^1 9800\pi(y^3-3y^2-y+3)\,\dee{y}\\ &=9800\pi\left[\frac{1}{4}y^4 - y^3-\frac{1}{2}y^2+3y\right]_0^1\\ &=17,150\pi \text{J} \end{align*}
