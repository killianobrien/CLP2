Footnote 1.8.3
The integral of secant played an important role in constructing accurate Mercator projection maps of the earth. See https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Integral_of_the_secant_function and https://arxiv.org/pdf/2204.11187.pdf. The method for evaluating the integral that is being presented in this example was invented by the Scottish mathematician James Gregory in 1668. There are also a number of other methods.
