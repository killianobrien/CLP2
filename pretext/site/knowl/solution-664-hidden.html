We apply the ratio test with \(a_n = \frac{(x - 2)^n}{n^2+1}\text{.}\)

\begin{align*} \lim_{n\rightarrow\infty}\Big|\frac{a_{n+1}}{a_n}\Big| &=\lim_{n\rightarrow\infty}\bigg|\frac{(x-2)^{n+1}}{(n+1)^2+1}\cdot \frac{n^2+1}{(x-2)^n}\bigg|\\ &=\lim_{n\rightarrow\infty}\frac{n^2+1}{(n+1)^2+1} |x-2|\\ &=\lim_{n\rightarrow\infty}\frac{1+1/n^2}{(1+1/n)^2+1/n^2} |x-2|\\ &=|x-2| \end{align*}

So, the series converges if \(|x-2| \lt 1\) and diverges if \(|x-2| \gt 1\text{.}\) That is, the radius of convergence is 1.
