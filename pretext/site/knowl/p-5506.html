(a) We know that the radius of convergence \(R\) obeys

\begin{gather*} \frac{1}{R} = \lim_{n\rightarrow\infty}\frac{a_{n+1}}{a_n} = \lim_{n\rightarrow\infty}\frac{n}{n+1}\ \frac{(n+1)a_{n+1}}{na_n} = 1 \frac{C}{C} = 1 \end{gather*}

because we are told that \(\lim\limits_{n\rightarrow\infty} na_n=C\text{.}\) So \(R=1\text{.}\)
