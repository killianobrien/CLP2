Now since our function, \(f(x)\) is differentiable we can apply one of the main theorems we learned in CLP-1 — the Mean Value Theorem  22 . The MVT implies that there exists a \(c\) between \({\underline x}_j\) and \({\overline x}_j\) such that
\begin{gather*} f({\overline x}_j)-f({\underline x}_j) =f'(c)\cdot [{\overline x}_j-{\underline x}_j] \end{gather*}
