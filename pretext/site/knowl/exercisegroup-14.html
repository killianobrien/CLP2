Exercise Group 1.5.2.7–13

Exercises — Stage 2
7 (✳)

Find the area of the region bounded by the graph of \(f (x) = \dfrac{1}{(2x-4)^2}\) and the \(x\)--axis between \(x = 0\) and \(x = 1\text{.}\)

8 (✳)

Find the area between the curves \(y=x\) and \(y=3x-x^2\text{,}\) by first identifying the points of intersection and then integrating.

9 (✳)

Calculate the area of the region enclosed by \(y = 2^x\) and \(y = \sqrt x+1\text{.}\)

10 (✳)

Find the area of the finite region bounded between the two curves \(y = \sqrt{2} \cos(\pi x/4)\) and \(y = |x|\text{.}\)

11 (✳)

Find the area of the finite region that is bounded by the graphs of \(f(x) = x^2\sqrt{x^3+1}\) and \(g(x) = 3x^2\text{.}\)

12 (✳)

Find the area to the left of the \(y\)--axis and to the right of the curve \(x=y^2+y\text{.}\)

13

Find the area of the finite region below \(y=\sqrt{9-x^2}\) and above both \(y=|x|\) and \(y=\sqrt{1-x^2}\text{.}\)
