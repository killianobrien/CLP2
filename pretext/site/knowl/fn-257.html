Footnote D.1.5
Notice that we have made a first approximation for \(\varphi(t_{n+1})\) by using Euler's method. Then improved Euler uses the first approximation to build a better approximation for \(\varphi(t_{n+1})\text{.}\) Building an approximation on top of another approximation does not always work, but it works very well here.in-context
