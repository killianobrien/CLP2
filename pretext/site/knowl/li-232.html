We match the terms in the definition with the terms in the problem:
\begin{align*} f(a+(i-1)\Delta x) & = \tan\left(\frac{\pi (i-1)}{20}\right)\\ f\left(a+(i-1)\frac{\pi}{20}\right) & = \tan\left((i-1)\frac{\pi }{20}\right) \end{align*}
So, we choose \(a=0\) and \(f(x) = \tan x\text{.}\)
