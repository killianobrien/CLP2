Equation C.2.1 Romberg integration

Let \(T(h)\) be the trapezoidal rule approximation, with step size \(h\text{,}\) to an integral \(I\text{.}\) The Romberg integration algorithm is

\begin{align*} T_1(h)&=\tfrac{4T(h/2)-T(h)}{3} \\ T_2(h)&=\tfrac{16T_1(h/2)-T_1(h)}{15} \\ T_3(h)&=\tfrac{64T_2(h/2)-T_2(h)}{63} \\ &\ \ \vdots \\ T_k(h)&=\tfrac{2^{2k} T_{k-1}(h/2)-T_{k-1}(h)}{2^{2k}-1} \\ &\ \ \vdots \end{align*}
