Using the Taylor series for \(\log(1+x)\text{:}\)

\begin{align*} \log(1+x)&=\sum_{n=1}^\infty (-1)^{n+1}\frac{x^n}{n}\\ \log(1.5)=\log\left(1+\frac12\right)&=\sum_{n=1}^\infty (-1)^{n+1}\frac{1}{n2^n} \end{align*}

Since this is an alternating series, the error involved in using the partial sum \(S_N\) is at most

\begin{equation*} |a_{N+1}|=\frac{1}{(N+1)2^{N+1}}. \end{equation*}
