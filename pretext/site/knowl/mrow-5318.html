\begin{align*} 0\leq a_n^2 & \leq a_n\qquad\text{for }\\ \end{align*}

By the direct comparison test,

\begin{align*} \sum_{n=N+1}^\infty a_n^2& \qquad\text{converges.}\\ \end{align*}

Since convergence doesn't depend on the first \(N\) terms of a series for any finite \(N\text{,}\)

\begin{align*} \sum_{n=1}^\infty a_n^2& \qquad\text{converges as well.} \end{align*}
