As a check, we observe that the derivative of the answer

\begin{align*} \diff{}{x} \left(\frac14 \frac x{\sqrt{x^2+4}} +C\right) &=\frac14\frac 1{\sqrt{x^2+4}} - \frac{1}{2\times 4}\frac{x(2x)}{{\big(x^2+4\big)}^{3/2}}\\ &=\frac{\frac{x^2}{4}+1-\frac{x^2}{4}}{{\big(x^2+4\big)}^{3/2}} =\frac{1}{{\big(x^2+4\big)}^{3/2}} \end{align*}

is exactly the integrand.
