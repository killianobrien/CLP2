The series \(\sum\limits_{n=1}^{\infty}\frac{(-1)^n}{9n+5}\) converges by the alternating series test. On the other hand the series \(\sum\limits_{n=1}^{\infty}\big|\frac{(-1)^n}{9n+5}\big| =\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\frac{1}{9n+5}\) diverges by the limit comparison test with \(b_n=\frac{1}{n}\text{.}\) So the given series is conditionally convergent.
