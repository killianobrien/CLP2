Since \(k\) runs from 0 to \(60,000\text{,}\) the total mass is given by

\begin{align*} \int_0^{60000} c\pi2^{-k/6000} \dee{k}&=c\pi\int_0^{60000} 2^{-k/6000} \dee{k}\\ \end{align*}
