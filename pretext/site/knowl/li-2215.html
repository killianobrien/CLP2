Item
In order for book #2 to not topple off of book #1, we need the centre of mass of book #2 to lie above book #1. That is, we need the \(x\)-coordinate of the centre mass of \(B_2\text{,}\) which is \(\bar X(B_2)=x_2-\frac{L}{2}\text{,}\) to obey
\begin{equation*} \bar X(B_2) \le x_1 \iff x_2-\frac{L}{2} \le x_1 \iff x_2\le x_1+\frac{L}{2} \end{equation*}
