Exercise 26

Suppose \(f(x)\) is continuous for all real numbers, and \(\displaystyle\int_1^\infty f(x) \dee{x}\) converges.

  1. If \(f(x)\) is odd, does \(\displaystyle\int_{-\infty\vphantom{\frac12}}^{-1} f(x) \dee{x}\) converge or diverge, or is there not enough information to decide?
  2. If \(f(x)\) is even, does \(\displaystyle\int_{-\infty}^\infty f(x) \dee{x}\) converge or diverge, or is there not enough information to decide?
