We also remark that this is a demonstration of the usefulness of integrals. We wanted to know how much of something there was, but the amount of that something was different everywhere: more in some places, less in others. Integration allowed us to account for this gradient. You've seen this behaviour exploited to find distances travelled, areas, volumes, and now mass.
