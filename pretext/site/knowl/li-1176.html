As our bottommost strip is at \(y=0\) and our topmost strip is at \(y=\frac{10}{3}\) (since at the top \(x=4\) and \(y= \frac{10}{\sqrt{25-x^2}} =\frac{10}{\sqrt{25-4^2}} =\frac{10}{3}\)), the volume is
\begin{align*} &\int _{5/2}^{10/3} \pi\Big(\frac{100}{y^2}-9\Big)\dee{y} +\int _ 0^{5/2}7 \pi\,\dee{y}\\ &=\pi{\Big[-\frac{100}{y}-9y\Big]}_{5/2}^{10/3} +\frac{35}{2}\pi\\ &=\pi \Big[-30+40-30+\frac{45}{2}\Big] +\frac{35}{2}\pi\\ &=20\pi \end{align*}
