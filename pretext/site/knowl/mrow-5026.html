\begin{align*} s_N&=\sum_{n=3}^N\left(\frac{\sin^2 n}{2^n}+\frac{\cos^2(n+1)}{2^{n+1}} \right)\\ &=\frac{\sin^23}{2^3} +\left( \sum_{n=4}^{N}\frac{1}{2^n} \right)+\frac{\cos^2(N+1)}{2^{N+1}}\\ \end{align*}

Using Definition 3.2.3, our series evaluates to:

\begin{align*} \lim_{N \to \infty}s_N &=\lim_{N \to \infty}\left[\frac{\sin^23}{2^3} +\left( \sum_{n=4}^{N}\frac{1}{2^n} \right)+\frac{\cos^2(N+1)}{2^{N+1}}\right]\\ &=\frac{\sin^23}{8}+\left[\lim_{N \to \infty}\frac{\cos^2(N+1)}{2^{N+1}}\right] +\sum_{n=4}^\infty \frac{1}{2^n}\\ \end{align*}

We evaluate the limit using the squeeze theorem; the series is geometric.

\begin{align*} &=\frac{\sin^23}{8}+0+\sum_{n=0}^\infty \frac{1}{2^{n+4}}\\ &=\frac{\sin^23}{8}+\frac{1}{2^4}\sum_{n=0}^\infty \frac{1}{2^{n}}\\ \end{align*}

Using Lemma 3.2.5,

\begin{align*} &=\frac{\sin^23}{8}+\frac{1}{2^4} \frac{1}{1-\frac12}\\ &=\frac{\sin^23}{8}+\frac{1}{8} \approx 0.1275 \end{align*}
