\begin{align*} \Delta W &=17,150\pi -\frac{2450}{9}\pi\left(8\pi-9\right)\\ &=2450\pi\left(7-\frac{8\pi-9}{9}\right)\\ &=2450\pi\left(8-\frac{8\pi}{9}\right)\\ &=19,600\pi\left(1-\frac{\pi}{9}\right)\\ \end{align*}

As a percentage of 17,150\(\pi\text{,}\) this is:

\begin{align*} \text{waste}&= \left(\frac{19,600\pi\left(1-\frac{\pi}{9}\right)}{17,150\pi}\right)\times 100\\ &=\frac{8}{7}\left(1-\frac{\pi}{9}\right)\times 100\approx 74\% \end{align*}
