\begin{align*} \lim_{n\to\infty} \bigg| \frac{a_{n+1}}{a_n} \bigg| &= \lim_{n\to\infty} \bigg| \frac{(x-1)^{n+1}}{2^{n+1}(n+3)}\ \frac{2^n(n+2)}{(x-1)^n} \bigg|\\ &= \lim_{n\to\infty} \frac{|x-1|}{2} \frac{n+2}{n+3}\\ &= \frac{|x-1|}{2} \lim_{n\to\infty} \frac{1+2/n}{1+3/n} = \frac{|x-1|}{2} \end{align*}
