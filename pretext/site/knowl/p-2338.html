\begin{align*} \frac{2}{x(x+1)}&=\frac{A}{x}+\frac{B}{x+1} = \frac{(A+B)x+A}{x(x+1)} \end{align*}

Matching coefficients

\begin{gather*} A+B=0,\ A=2 \implies \color{red}{A=2,\quad B=-2} \end{gather*}

and the integral

\begin{align*} \int\frac{2}{x(x+1)} \dee{x}&=\int\left(\frac{2}{x}-\frac{2}{x+1}\right) \dee{x}=2\log|x|-2\log|x+1|+C\\ &=2\log\left| \frac{x}{x+1}\right|+C \end{align*}
