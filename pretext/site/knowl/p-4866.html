Assume that

\begin{equation*} L = \lim_{n\to\infty}\root{n}\of{\big|a_n\big|} \end{equation*}

exists or is \(+\infty\text{.}\)

  1. If \(L \lt 1\text{,}\) then \(\sum\limits_{n=1}^\infty a_n\) converges.
  2. If \(L \gt 1\text{,}\) or \(L=+\infty\text{,}\) then \(\sum\limits_{n=1}^\infty a_n\) diverges.
