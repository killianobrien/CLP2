Exercise 18

A force acts on a spring, and the spring stretches and contracts. The distance beyond its natural length at time \(t\) is \(f(t) = \sin\left(t\pi\right)\) cm, where \(t\) is measured in seconds. The spring constant is 3 N/cm.

  1. What is the force exerted by the spring at time \(t\text{,}\) if it obeys Hooke's law?
  2. Find the average of the force exerted by the spring from \(t=0\) to \(t=6\text{.}\)
  3. Find the root mean square of the force exerted by the spring from \(t=0\) to \(t=6\text{.}\)
