Solution 2: Alternatively, we can use the ratio test with \(a_n=24n^2 e^{-n^3}\text{.}\) We calculate

\begin{align*} \lim_{n\to\infty} \left|\frac{a_{n+1}}{a_n}\right| &= \lim_{n\to\infty} \left|\frac{24 (n+1)^2e^{-(n+1)^3}}{24 n^2e^{-n^3}} \right|\\ &= \lim_{n\to\infty} \left( \frac{(n+1)^2}{n^2} \frac{e^{n^3}}{e^{(n+1)^3}} \right)\\ &= \lim_{n\to\infty} \left(1+\frac{1}{n}\right)^2 e^{-(3n^2+3n+1)} = 1\cdot0=0 \lt 1, \end{align*}

and therefore the series converges absolutely.
