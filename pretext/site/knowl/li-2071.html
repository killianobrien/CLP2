Item 3.1.2.30.b
The most-used word in a language is \(w_1\text{,}\) while the \(n\)-th most used word in a language is \(w_n\text{.}\) So, we re-state the law as:
\begin{equation*} f_1=nf_n \end{equation*}
Then we can rewrite this fomula a little more naturally as \(f_n=\frac{1}{n}f_1\text{.}\)
