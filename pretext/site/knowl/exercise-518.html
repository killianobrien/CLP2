\begin{gather*} \diff{B}{t}=\big(0.06+0.02\sin t\big)B \end{gather*}
  1. Solve this differential equation for \(B\) as a function of \(t\text{.}\)
  2. If the initial investment is \(\$1000\text{,}\) what will the balance be at the end of two years?
