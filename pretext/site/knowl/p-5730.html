Substituting \(y=2x\) into \(\displaystyle \frac{1}{1-y} = \sum\limits_{n=0}^\infty y^n\) (which is valid for all \(-1\lt y \lt 1\)) gives

\begin{gather*} f(x) = \frac{1}{2x-1} = -\frac{1}{1-2x} =-\sum_{n=0}^\infty (2x)^n =-\sum_{n=0}^\infty 2^nx^n \end{gather*}

for all \(-\tfrac{1}{2}\lt x\lt \tfrac{1}{2}\text{.}\)
