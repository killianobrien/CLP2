The theorem says that any continuous function on a closed interval must attain a minimum and maximum at least once. In this situation this implies that for any continuous function \(f(x)\text{,}\) there are \(x_{j-1}\le {\overline x}_j, {\underline x}_j\le x_j\) such that \(f({\underline x}_j)\le f(x) \le f({\overline x}_j)\) for all \(x_{j-1}\le x\le x_j\text{.}\)
