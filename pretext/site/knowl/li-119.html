Let \(f({\overline x}_j)\) and \(f({\underline x}_j)\) be the largest and smallest values  21  of \(f(x)\) for \(x_{j-1}\le x\le x_j\text{.}\) Then the true area is bounded by
\begin{gather*} f({\underline x}_j)[x_j-x_{j-1}] \leq A_j \leq f({\overline x}_j)[x_j-x_{j-1}]. \end{gather*}
