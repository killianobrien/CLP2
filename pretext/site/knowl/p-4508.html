Alternately, we can make use of the limit \(\lim\limits_{x \to 0}\frac{\sin x}{x}=1\text{.}\) Let \(x=\frac{2\pi}{n}\text{.}\) Note if \(n \to \infty\text{,}\) then \(x \to 0\text{.}\)

\begin{align*} \lim_{n \to \infty}A_n&=\lim_{n \to \infty}\frac{n}{2}\sin\left(\frac{2\pi}{n}\right) = \lim_{n \to \infty}\frac{\pi}{\frac{2\pi}{n}}\sin\left(\frac{2\pi}{n}\right)\\ &=\lim_{x \to 0}\pi\frac{\sin x}{x} =\pi\times 1=\pi \end{align*}
