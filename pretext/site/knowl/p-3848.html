First, let's use our horizontal slices  1  to find the area of our region, \(A\text{.}\)

\begin{align*} A&=\int_{-\pi/2}^{\pi/2} \big( (\textcolor{blue}{2-\sin y}) - (\textcolor{red}{\sin y})\big)\,\dee{y}=\int_{-\pi/2}^{\pi/2}\big(2-2\sin y\big)\,\dee{y}\\ &=\Big[2y+2\cos y\Big]_{-\pi/2}^{\pi/2} = \left(\pi+0\right) - \left(-\pi+0\right)=2\pi \end{align*}
