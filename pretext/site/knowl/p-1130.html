The top and bottom boundaries of the specified region are \(y=T(x)=x\) and \(y=B(x)=x^3-x\text{,}\) respectively. So,

\begin{align*} {\rm Area} &= \int_0^{\sqrt{2}}\big[T(x)-B(x)\big]\dee{x} = \int_0^{\sqrt{2}}\big[x-(x^3-x)\big]\dee{x} \\ &= \int_0^{\sqrt{2}} \big[2x-x^3\big] \dee{x} \end{align*}
