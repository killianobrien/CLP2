As a check, we observe that the derivative of the answer

\begin{align*} \diff{}{x}\left(-\frac{1}{16}\frac{\sqrt{x^2+16}}{x}+C\right) &=\frac{1}{16}\frac{\sqrt{x^2+16}}{x^2} -\frac{1}{16}\frac{x}{x\sqrt{x^2+16}}\\ & =\frac{1}{16}\frac{(x^2+16)-x^2}{x^2\sqrt{x^2+16}}\\ &=\frac{1}{x^2\sqrt{x^2+16}} \end{align*}

is exactly the integrand.
