\begin{align*} \sum_{n=0}^\infty x^n&=\frac{1}{1-x}\\ \end{align*}

Differentiating both sides with respect to \(x\text{:}\)

\begin{align*} \sum_{n=0}^\infty nx^{n-1}&=\frac{1}{(1-x)^2}\\ \sum_{n=1}^\infty nx^{n-1}&=\frac{1}{(1-x)^2}\\ \end{align*}

Multiplying both sides by \(x\text{:}\)

\begin{align*} \sum_{n=1}^\infty nx^{n}&=\frac{x}{(1-x)^2} \end{align*}
