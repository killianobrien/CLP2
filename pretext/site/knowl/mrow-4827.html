\begin{align*} \lim_{n \to \infty}n\left[\left(2+\frac1n\right)^{100}-2^{100}\right]&= \lim_{h \to 0}\frac{\left(2+h\right)^{100}-2^{100}}{h}\\ \end{align*}

This reminds us of the definition of a derivative.

\begin{align*} \diff{}{x}\left\{x^{100}\right\}&=\lim_{h \to 0}\frac{(x+h)^{100}-x^{100}}{h} \end{align*}
