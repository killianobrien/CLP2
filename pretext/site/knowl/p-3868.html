(a) The two curves cross at points \((x,y)\) that satisfy both \(y=x^2\) and \(y = 6-x\text{,}\) and hence

\begin{gather*} x^2 = 6-x \iff x^2+x-6=0 \iff (x+3)(x-2)=0 \end{gather*}
