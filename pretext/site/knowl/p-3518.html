By definition, the average value is:

\begin{align*} \frac{1}{2\pi}\int_0^{2\pi}\cos^2x\,\dee{x} \amp=\frac{1}{2\pi}\cdot\frac{1}{2}\int_0^{2\pi}\big(\cos(2x)+1\big)\,\dee{x}\\ \amp=\frac{1}{4\pi}\Big[\frac{\sin(2x)}{2}+x\Big]_0^{2\pi}\\ \amp=\frac{1}{4\pi}\cdot 2\pi =\frac{1}{2} \end{align*}
