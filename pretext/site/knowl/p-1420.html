Let \(\cR\) be the plane region bounded by \(x=0,\ x=1,\ y=0\) and \(y=c\sqrt{1+x^2}\text{,}\) where \(c\ge 0\) is a constant.

  1. Find the volume \(V_1\) of the solid obtained by revolving \(\cR\) about the \(x\)--axis.
  2. Find the volume \(V_2\) of the solid obtained by revolving \(\cR\) about the \(y\)--axis.
  3. If \(V_1=V_2\text{,}\) what is the value of \(c\text{?}\)
