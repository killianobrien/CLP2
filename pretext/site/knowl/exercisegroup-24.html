A reduction formula.

  1. Let \(n\) be a positive integer with \(n\ge 2\text{.}\) Derive the reduction formula
    \begin{equation*} \int\tan^n(x)\,\dee{x}=\frac{\tan^{n-1}(x)}{n-1} -\int\tan^{n-2}(x)\,\dee{x}. \end{equation*}
  2. Calculate \(\displaystyle\int_0^{\pi/4}\tan^6(x)\,\dee{x}\text{.}\)
