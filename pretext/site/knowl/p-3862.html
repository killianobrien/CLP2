Vertical strips: The equation of the top of the region is \(y=T(x)=\dfrac{8}{x^3}\) and the equation of the bottom of the region is \(y=B(x)=0\text{.}\) Using vertical strips, as in the figure on the left above, the \(y\)-coordinate of the centre of mass is

\begin{align*} \bar y & = \frac{1}{2A}\int_1^\infty \big[T(x)^2-B(x)^2\big]\,\dee{x}\\ &= \frac{1}{8}\int_1^\infty \bigg( \frac{8}{x^3} \bigg)^2 \,\dee{x}\\ &= \lim_{t\to\infty} \bigg( \int_1^t \frac{8}{x^6}\,\dee{x} \bigg)\\ &= \lim_{t\to\infty} \bigg[ {-}\frac{8}{5x^5}\bigg]_1^t\\ &= \lim_{t\to\infty} \bigg[ {-}\frac{8}{5t^5} + \frac{8}{5\times1^5} \bigg] = \frac{8}{5} \end{align*}
