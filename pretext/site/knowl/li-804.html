As our bottommost strip is at \(y=0\) and our topmost strip is at \(y=\frac{\pi}{4}\) (since at the top \(x=1\) and \(x=\tan y\)), the total
\begin{align*} \text{Volume} &= \int _0^{\frac{\pi}{4}} \pi(1-\tan^2 y)\dee{y} = \int _0^{\frac{\pi}{4}} \pi(2-\sec^2 y)\dee{y}\\ &=\pi\big[2y -\tan y\big]_0^{\frac{\pi}{4}}\\ &= \frac{\pi^2}{2}-\pi \end{align*}
