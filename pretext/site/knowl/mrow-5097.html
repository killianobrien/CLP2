\begin{align*} \left|\frac{a_{n+1}}{a_n}\right| &= \left|\frac{(-3)^{n+1} \sqrt{n+2} (2n+3) x^{n+1} }{(-3)^n \sqrt{n+1} (2n+5) x^n} \right|\\ &= |-3| \cdot \frac{\sqrt{n+2}}{\sqrt{n+1}} \cdot \frac{2n+3}{2n+5} \cdot |x|\\ \end{align*}

So in the limit as \(n \to \infty\) we are left with

\begin{align*} \lim_{n\to\infty} \left|\frac{a_{n+1}}{a_n}\right| &= 3 |x| \end{align*}
