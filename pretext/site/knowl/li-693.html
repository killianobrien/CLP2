As our bottommost strip is at \(y=0\) and our topmost strip is at \(y=\log 2\) (since at the top \(x=2\) and \(x=e^y\)), the total
\begin{align*} \text{Volume} &= \int _0^{\log 2} \pi\big(4-e^{2y}\big)\dee{y} =\pi\big[4y -e^{2y}/2\big]_0^{\log 2}\\ &=\pi\Big[4\log 2 - 2 +\frac{1}{2}\Big] =\pi\Big[4\log 2 - \frac{3}{2}\Big] \end{align*}
Using a calculator, we see this is approximately \(3.998\text{.}\)
