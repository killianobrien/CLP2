First, we'll develop some intuition. For very large \(n\)

\begin{align*} \left|\frac{\sqrt{n}\cos(n)}{n^2-1}\right| &\approx \left|\frac{\sqrt{n}\cos(n)}{n^2}\right| = \left|\frac{\cos(n)}{n^{3/2}}\right| \le \frac{1}{n^{3/2}} \end{align*}

since \(\left|\cos(n)\right| \leq 1\) for all \(n\text{.}\) By the \(p\)-test, which is in ExampleÂ 3.3.6, the series \(\displaystyle \sum_{n=5}^\infty \frac{1}{n^p}\) converges for all \(p \gt 1\text{.}\) So we would expect the given series to converge absolutely.
