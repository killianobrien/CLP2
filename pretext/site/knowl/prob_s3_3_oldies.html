Exercise 3

Suppose there is some threshold after which a person is considered old, and before which they are young.

Let Olaf be an old person, and let Yuan be a young person.

  1. Suppose I am older than Olaf. Am I old?
  2. Suppose I am younger than Olaf. Am I old?
  3. Suppose I am older than Yuan. Am I young?
  4. Suppose I am younger than Yuan. Am I young?
