\begin{align*} \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\frac{1}{2^n}+\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\frac{1}{3^n}&= \sum_{n=0}^{\infty}\frac{1}{2}\cdot\frac{1}{2^n}+\sum_{n=0}^{\infty}\frac{1}{3}\cdot\frac{1}{3^n}\\ &=\frac{1}{2}\cdot\frac{1}{1-\frac12}+\frac{1}{3}\cdot\frac{1}{1-\frac13}\\ &=1+\frac12=\frac32 \end{align*}
