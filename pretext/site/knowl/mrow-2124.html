\begin{align*} &\diff{}{x}\left\{\log\left| \dfrac{1+x}{\sqrt{1-x^2}}\right|\right\}\\ &\hskip0.5in=\diff{}{x}\left\{\log|1+x| - \frac{1}{2}\log|1+x|- \frac{1}{2}\log|1-x|\right\}\\ &\hskip0.5in=\frac{1}{1+x} - \frac{1/2}{1+x}+ \frac{1/2}{1-x}\\ &\hskip0.5in= \frac{1/2}{1+x}+ \frac{1/2}{1-x}\\ &\hskip0.5in=\frac{1}{1-x^2} \end{align*}
