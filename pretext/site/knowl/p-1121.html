We are finding the area in the interval from \(x=0\) to \(x=\frac{\pi}{2}\text{.}\) Since we're taking \(n=5\) rectangles, our rectangles cover the following intervals:

\begin{equation*} \left[0,\frac{\pi}{10}\right],\quad \left[\frac{\pi}{10},\frac{\pi}{5}\right],\quad \left[\frac{\pi}{5},\frac{3\pi}{10}\right],\quad \left[\frac{3\pi}{10},\frac{2\pi}{5}\right],\quad \left[\frac{2\pi}{5},\frac{\pi}{2}\right]. \end{equation*}
